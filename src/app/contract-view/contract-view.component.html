Alternative Dispute Dialogue E-Resolution (ADDER)
Alternatives to litigation
Alternative Dispute Resolution can often be cheaper, quicker, and simpler than pursuing a matter in court. This approach can be more flexible and less formal.
There are several options for this:
- Mediation – a discussion of the case between yourself, the other side, and a third party who helps you to try reach a solution. If you would like to continue with this please click here.
- Adjudication – an independent person considers your dispute and makes a decision for you. For a list of providers of this, please click here.
- Arbitration – this is similar to adjudication, but the decision made is binding, and the process is slightly more formal. For more information on this please click here.
- Ombudsman – if your complaint is with a public body, if may be worth approaching the ombudsman. For more information please click here.
